Maffugoe

This is a mamalian species native to the mountainous and woodland reigons of Awkgeo, the majority live in the temperate area of Silagate Mountainrange but they also reach out to the outermost edges of the continent. This species is split into 3 known subspecies and they are all under an old-age style Kingship consisting of one individual from the largest subspecie. They are still preyed upon by predators and they live on a diet consisting of invertibrates and fruit. They are commonly known as merchants and builders who trade and borrow from other species. They have recently discovered space travel and inhabit one other planet. Their main focus of science is neuro-science and the use of electricity.

Nyphae

A highly developed insectoid race that inhabits almost all explored reigions of Awkgeo, they have cities complete with modes of travel placed in the more tropical climates. They are close associates and negotiators with Maffugoe kings and other species on Awkgeo and other planets, often sharing their resources or wisdom with others. They also help in preventing wars and resolving conflict, as they have declared themselves a peaceful race. It is unknown weather they are native to this planet as they are distributed widley. Their technology is highest developed in space travel, city building and communication. they channel a lot of their work using other species and have their own brand of service animals artificialy created by them, this highlights their knowlage in genetic engineering.
